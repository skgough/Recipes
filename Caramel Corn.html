12 to 15 cups popped corn
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup light corn syrup
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
  1. Divide corn into 2 ungreased sheet pans.
  2. Heat sugar, butter, corn syrup, and salt, stirring occasionally, until bubbling around the edges of the pot. Set a timer for 5 minutes and stir constantly.
  3. After the timer goes off, remove from heat and stir in 1/2 tsp baking soda, pour over popcorn, and stir to evenly distribute before the caramel cools too much.
  4. Bake in 200 F oven for one hour stirring every 15 minutes.
