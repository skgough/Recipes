Bolognese SauceHome

4 servings
2 hours
1 large white onion
3 stalks celery
3 large carrots
3 tbsp tomato paste
1 lb ground beef
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup red wine
28 oz can San Marzano tomatoes
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
3 bay leaves
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
2 tsp Italian seasoning
2 tbsp butter
  1. Chop up the onion, celery and carrots and pulse in a food processor until minced. Cook in olive oil in a dutch oven for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Stir in 3 tbsp tomato paste and throw in the garlic once the paste has started to darken. Once the garlic is fragrant, throw in the meat and cook until fully browned. Pour in 1 cup wine and scrape the bottom with a wooden spoon to deglaze. Cook until all the alcohol evaporates.
  3. Puree tomatoes and pour into the pot. Add 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce, 3 bay leaves, 1/2 tsp cayenne, and 2 tsp Italian seasoning. Cover and simmer for 1 hour, or to desired thickness.
  4. To finish, toss in the butter. Add salt and pepper to taste.
