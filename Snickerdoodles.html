1 cup shortening
1 1/2 cups sugar
2  eggs
2 3/4 cups all purpose flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
  1. Cream shortening and sugar, add eggs and mix until combined.
  2. Sift flour, baking powder and salt, add to creamed mixture and mix thoroughly.
  3. Chill dough at least one hour.
  4. Preheat oven to 400 F.
  5. Form into ~1 inch balls and roll in a 1:3 mixture of cinnamon and sugar. Place two inches apart on a baking sheet and bake for 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned and the tops have begun to crack.
