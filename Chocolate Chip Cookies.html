Chocolate Chip CookiesHome

3 dozen
30 minutes
½ cup unsalted butter softened
½ cup butter flavored shortening
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup light brown sugar packed
2 tsp pure vanilla extract
2 large eggs
3 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
½ tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
12 oz (1 bag) chocolate chips
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Line a baking pan with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. Cream together butter and sugars until combined. Beat in eggs and vanilla until fluffy. Set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl mix flour, baking soda, salt, and baking powder. Mix in the dry ingredients into wet until combined. Add half of the 12 oz package of chocolate chips and mix well.
  4. Refrigerate 1 hour.
  5. Roll dough into walnut sized balls. Bake in preheated oven for approximately 8-10 minutes. Take them out when they are just barely starting to turn brown.
