Arroz con Tomate

4 servings
30 minutes
  • 1 cuplong grain white rice
  • 1 cupwater
  • 8oz cantomato sauce
  • 2 tspCalde de Tomate
  • 1/2 tspcumin
  • 1 tbspsriracha or other mild hot sauce

Brown the rice on high heat in a nonstick pan (that has a lid) in vegetable oil until it begins to turn opaque white, then golden. Take off the heat, and CAREFULLY pour in 1 cup of water. This will produce steam so make sure you don't get burned.

After the water has cooled off the pan, stir in the rest of the ingredients. Calde de Tomate is actually pretty easy to find if you look in the international section of your grocery store, but if you can't find it use normal bouillon or chicken stock.

Return to a simmer and cover, and let it cook for 20 minutes. Take off the heat and let sit for 5 minutes to absorb the rest of the water, fluff and serve.
