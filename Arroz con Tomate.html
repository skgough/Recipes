Arroz con Tomate Home

4 servings
30 minutes
1 cup basmati rice
1 cup water
2 tsp calde de tomate
8oz can tomato sauce
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp oregano
1 tbsp Valentina or other mild hot sauce
  1. Rinse the rice until water runs clear. Toast the rice on high heat, constantly stirring, in lidded nonstick pan until it begins to turn opaque white, then golden. Take off the heat, and pour in 1 cup of water.
  2. Stir in 2tsp calde de tomate, tomato sauce, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp oregano and 1 tbsp Valentina.
  3. Return to a simmer and cover, and let it cook for 20 minutes. Take off the heat and let sit for 5 minutes to absorb the rest of the water, fluff and serve.
