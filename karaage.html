4 lbs chicken
3 cups AP flour
1 3/4 cups potato starch
1 tbsp salt
2 tsp baking powder
1 2/3 tsp garlic powder
2 1/4 tsp onion powder
1-2 cups water
  1. Heat oil in a large heavy-bottomed pot to 350°F (175°C) 
  2. Season both sides of the chicken with salt.
  3. Toss chicken in ~1 cup of the flour to start a base coat.
  4. Create the breading mix: combine the 2 cups flour, potato starch, salt, baking powder, garlic powder, and onion powder. 
  5. Add 1/3 of dry breading mix into a 3rd bowl & add 1-2 cups water to create a light slurry.
  6. Now mix about ⅓ of the wet slurry with the rest of the dry breading to create a craggy mix for coating.
  7. Dip the chicken into the remaining wet slurry, then coat with the craggy mix.
  8. Add wings to the 350F oil in 2-3 batches. Fry for 11-12 minutes until golden brown
